### 🏛️ 1. Perplexity "Personal Computer" Kya Hai?
Ye Perplexity ka shift hai "Search Engine" se ek **"Digital Worker"** ki taraf.

* **Desktop Integration**: Ye app aapke local desktop environment (files, apps, browser) ka access leti hai. 
* **Action Engine**: Screenshot mein dekho, ye agent bol raha hai: *"On it — I'll pull the files, check the brand guidelines, and push to Vercel"*. Iska matlab ye sirf text generate nahi kar raha, balki aapke system par **Actions** le raha hai.
* **Always-On Worker**: Jab ise Mac Mini ke saath pair karte hain, toh ye 24*7 digital worker ban jata hai jo aapke business aur life ko manage karta hai, aur aap ise apne iPhone se control kar sakte ho.


---

### 🧬 2. Technical Audit: Cluaiz vs. Perplexity
Bhai, humein researcher mode mein ye samajhna hoga ki hum inse kahan alag hain:

* **Cloud vs. Silicon Mastery**: Perplexity ka "Personal Computer" asaliyat mein **Cloud-First** hai. Wo aapke screen ka data cloud par bhejte hain, wahan LLM "Dimaag" chalata hai aur wapas command bhejta hai. 
* **Archer V8 Advantage**: Hamara goal **"Sovereign Silicon Mastery"** hai.md. Hum wahi "Digital Worker" capabilities local hardware par **0.000ms latency** aur **Assembly-level precision** ke saath dena chahte hain, bina data cloud par bheje.md.
* **The "Agentic" Layer**: Perplexity yahan ek "Agentic Gateway" hai, bilkul **OpenClaw** ki tarah, jo interface aur automation par focus karta hai.
